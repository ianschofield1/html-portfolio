The Best Movies of All Time

Ian's Top 3 Movies to Watch

Interstellar

This movie is put together incredibly well and graps all of the viewers emotions in just 2 hours. From the dreadful pain of choosing saving the world over family to the multilayered aspects of travelling through interstellar space, this movie does it all. It is number one on my list!

The Hangover

A classic watch that will have in tears by the time you're finished. This film captures a bachelor party gone wrong...or right depending on your perspective.

White Chicks

This is also a classic watch that has some of the funniest moments in comedy history. It may be the most rewatched comedy of all time.
